**Context**: Emma prefers to focus quietly on activities alone, especially in busy places like the park.

**Feeling**: Sometimes, when someone tries to help me without asking, I feel overwhelmed and anxious.

**Strategy**: It's okay to tell someone, "I want to do this by myself right now." I can also take a deep breath to help me feel calm.

**Positive Outcome**: When I tell people what I need, they understand better. This helps me feel more comfortable and happy.

**Practice Phrase**: "Please, I'd like to do it by myself." 

This simple structure helps reinforce a clear understanding of personal boundaries and self-regulation. The story mirrors her preference for solitary activity and empowers her to communicate her needs effectively.
